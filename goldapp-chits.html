How Gold Chits Work

A modern twist on traditional chit funds, but your money is converted to digital gold every month.

1
Join a Chit
Choose a chit plan that fits your budget and timeline. Pay the first installment to secure your spot.
2
Monthly Contributions
Every month, all members contribute their fixed amount. Money is pooled together and converted to gold.
3
Monthly Auctions
Each month, members can bid for the pooled amount. Highest bidder gets the full amount early.
4
Gold Accumulation
Your contributions are converted to digital gold at current rates. Gold is stored securely in your account.
5
Flexible Usage
At any time, redeem your gold for cash, convert to jewelry, or continue holding as investment.
6
Completion
After the chit period ends, you'll have accumulated significant gold through systematic saving.

Why Choose Gold Chits?

🏆 Disciplined Savings

Commit to regular savings with the motivation of group participation and gold accumulation.

💰 Early Access Opportunity

Bid in auctions to get early access to pooled funds for urgent financial needs.

🔒 Gold Security

Your money is converted to physical gold, providing hedge against inflation and currency fluctuation.
